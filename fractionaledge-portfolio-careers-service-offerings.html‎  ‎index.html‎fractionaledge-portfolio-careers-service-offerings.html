Portfolio Careers

A comprehensive program for helping versatile professionals build multi-stream businesses that provide income diversity, flexibility, and scalability. Research shows the most successful portfolio careerists combine client work with digital products and content.

Three Business Models

The Consultant Model

Fastest path to revenue. Monetize existing skills through freelance projects, fractional roles, or advisory positions. Low risk, quick cash flow, but income tied directly to time invested.

  • Hourly or retainer-based client work
  • Project-based consulting engagements
  • Fractional executive roles
  • Corporate training and workshops

The Creator Model

Scale your impact and income. Convert expertise into digital products that sell repeatedly. Higher upfront effort, slower initial revenue, but creates lasting passive income streams.

  • Online courses and training programs
  • Membership communities and newsletters
  • Books, ebooks, and publications
  • Podcasts and video content

The Hybrid Model

Best of both worlds. Combine consulting for stable cash flow with creator products for scale and freedom. Most sustainable long-term approach, but requires careful time management.

  • 80/20 consulting to product ratio
  • Consulting fuels product development
  • Products fund creative freedom
  • Gradual shift toward products over time
